Project Description

For some people music is a important part of life ...for others its much much more.For them Music is Life.

With the people being occupied with their day to day jobs, music sharing and discovery has not only become difficult but also has lost its personal essence .

With SUZIK we will revolutionize the way people share and discover new music.

SUZIK is built on a simple concept that we humans are very dependent on our social circle,so dependent that we cannot live without it.So why not integrate it with our music?

With this app you will be able to instantly share and discover music among your loved ones

Customer Information

Our target customers are mainly youngsters(students etc) for whom music and their social circle are an integral part of their life.

Project Details

Suzik is an app wich seamlessly integrates the influence of the social aspect of your life with your music thereby keeping you more connected with your social group.

Suzik is the ultimate music destination for any android user.

It has the following features-

1. TIMELINE:

A intuitive user interface to discover music among your friends.Timeline intelligently monitors the music listening and downloading patterns of your friends and recommends refreshing music which you always wanted.Since music is nothing but personal ,timeline can be personalized to discover a selected genre and to filter out people used for music discovery.

2. QUICK SHARE AND ACCESS:

Getting a song that you liked from your friend has always been a tedious job.Well ,with SUZIK your friends music library is one touch away.Share and access each other music libraries and instantly listen to it or download it.

3. ONE TOUCH DOWNLOAD AND PLAY:

Want to listen to a song? Just touch it.

Want to download a song? Just tap it.

You favourite music is one touch away from being played or downloaded.

4. SYNC AND STORE MUSIC:

SUZIK seamlessly sync your music to any device it has been installed into.You can access all the music where ever you go.
